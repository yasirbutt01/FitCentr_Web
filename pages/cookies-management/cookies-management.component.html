Cookies in FitCentr

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet, or computer when you visit FitCenter. We use cookies to make FitCenter work, to have it operate smoothly, and to collect certain information.

Cookie settings

We use 3 types of cookies. Enable those you allow us to use.

Cookies that help us to improve the product

We use anonymous tracking cookies to collect information on how you use FitCenter. It helps us make FitCenter better. Tracking cookies do not identify anyone. They store anonym yed information about FitCenter pages you visited, time your spent on them, and page objects you clicked on. We do not share the collected data with third-party services.

Cookies that help with our communications and marketing

These cookies collect information about your FitCenter preferences and help us provide you with personal recommendations.

Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies enable FitCenter core functions such as security, network management and accessibility. These cookies always need to be on.
